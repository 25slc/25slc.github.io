Youth Faculty 6: The Renaissance of the Arts

Under Youth Assembly 2: The Arts

This faculty has examined into the applications of arts in various aspects of life and found out that art does serve the human beings economically, socially and politically. 

We were able to identify the issue of diminishing in some art industries due to certain social mindsets and hence the lack of awareness and exposure to these art forms. 

The solutions provided by this faculty are closely intertwined with changes to be made in the education system. Some of the solutions include channel resources to schools to train the teachers, so that arts can be implemented into daily teaching by the mean of multimedia and through various activities. Others include providing more opportunities for students to be exposed to arts and more opportunities for arts students. 

The Lens

In the video produced by our faculty, the audience will be able to view the issue in the perspective of a reporter, interviewing the respective stakeholders including the students, parents and teachers. Our solution to increase awareness and exposure will also be reflected in the video. Hope you will enjoy it!
