Youth Faculty 7: Applied Learning

Under Youth Assembly 3: Holistic Education

Hello we are youth faculty 7 and the topic we were working on is applied learning!Applied learning refers to an educational approach where students learn by engaging in direct application of skills, theories and models. The applied learning activity can occur outside of the traditional classroom experience and/or be embedded as part of a course.

Some problems are:

  1. Takes attention away from national examinations/learning fundamental knowledge
  2. Lack of resources in developing countries
  3. Problems regarding the grading system:
  4. It would be difficult to grade students fairly
  5. Standardisation of teaching is required
  6. Problems regarding people’s mindsets:
  7. Students do not see the importance of applied learning

Some solutions are:

  1. Start with a new batch of students. (And for the current batch..) Use applied learning in increasing amounts, starting from preschool and working up to higher levels of education.
    2. (parental solutions)
  2. Use applied learning in increasing amounts, starting from preschool and working up to higher levels of education.
  3. Use both applied learning and conventional learning styles in conjunction
  4. Include applied learning questions in exams
  5. Value based applied learning - in preschools/lower primary
  6. Knowledge based applied learning - secondary schools all the way to undergrads
  7. The level of understanding should be increased and the questions should be asked on the concepts
  8. The way of marking should be subjective and should not be strictly restricted to the textbook.
