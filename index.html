About SLC

The Student Leaders Convention (SLC) is an annual event that serves as an avenue for student leaders from across the globe to come together and discuss pertinent issues in our present world and communities. The convention is an open platform for student leaders to put aside their differences, and gain insight to empower themselves with the confidence and strength to make a positive change back home. It has continually evolved since 1999 from a one-day local event to the four-day international convention it is today. The Theme for the 25th SLC is "Education: Transcending Boundaries, Pioneering Change."

Theme

Education: Transcending Boundaries, Pioneering Change.

Education is essential in changing the lives of individuals and empowering them to create change. It allows people to transcend cultural, socioeconomic and political barriers, thus revolutionising society. Yet, even with the multitude of benefits that education brings, certain problems have continued to plague countries in the successful implementation of effective education. In this rapidly globalising world, coupled with the advent of the Fourth Industrial Revolution, it is imperative that education evolves along with the ever-changing society so as to remain relevant in today’s world. In the 25th Student Leaders Convention, delegates will endeavour to attempt to resolve such issues.

Our Guest of Honour

Ms Indranee Rajah is the Minister in the Prime Minister’s Office. She is also Second Minister for Finance and Education.
